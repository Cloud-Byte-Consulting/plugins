# Research division provenance and publication review

The five research-division plugins were imported from the user-provided Git bundle
`research-division-plugins.bundle`. The bundle contains six commits based on this repository's
history: one commit for each plugin and one marketplace-registration commit. Cloud Byte Consulting
is the recorded author and publisher in that history.

The repository's Apache-2.0 license applies to the implementations published here. Product,
project, framework, and report names in the skills identify interoperability targets or cited
concepts; their inclusion does not relicense third-party documentation, software, or datasets.

Before publication, the imported material was reviewed and revised for:

- Kubernetes and Ray credential boundaries, tenant egress isolation, and privileged debugging.
- Privacy-safe inference capture and authorized red-team operation.
- Correct supervised-fine-tuning loss masking and distributed-workload scheduling semantics.
- Empirical robustness claims and white-box versus hosted-model evaluation boundaries.
- Object-store authorization, field-level sensitivity classification, non-leaky catalog search,
  and byte-reproducible materialized training inputs.
- MCP authorization enforcement and versioned external benchmark data.

The embedded platform-maturity percentages are explicitly labeled as a historical Volume 3
snapshot. Maintainers must retrieve and cite the latest report before making current percentile
claims. Future updates should preserve source/version labels for unstable benchmarks and SDK advice.
